When introducing new sound, keep together:
- new sound
- spelling of that new sound
- words that use the new sound
- sentences that use those words

When introducing new word, keep together:
- reading of word in sentence
- writing of word in sentence
- speaking of word in sentence
- hearing of word in sentence

Things to introduce early:
- "advanced" grammar

Things not to keep together:
- lists of related words (e.g. red, yellow, green, blue, white, black)

What's missing = fun/salience:
- passion-related words: wanting something, thinking, complexity
- words related to my field of work
- texting/social-media idioms
- variety of speakers/accents
